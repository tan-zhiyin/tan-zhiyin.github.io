The Midnight Gaze

Author: Zhiyin Tan
Date: 03 Jun 2023

Introduction:
In the quiet hush of midnight, the artist is entranced by the sight of two cats: Qing, an endearing mix of Neva Masquerade Siberian and Ragdoll, and Gao, his captivating elder brother of British Shorthair breed.

Their alternating gazes, curious yet affectionate, switch between the artist and the insects attracted to the ambient light, infusing the night with a sense of unexpected companionship.

This serene nocturnal scene cultivates a soothing tranquility that endures, lingering even as dawn ushers in a new day.

Genesis of Neues Dawn

Author: Zhiyin Tan
Date: 10 Mar 2020

Introduction:
This painting captures a poignant moment of introspection and transition. A figure seated on a carpet under dim light casts a glance over her shoulder, her eyes reflecting a determined resignation to a world she is ready to leave behind.

While the face does not mirror the artist, the gaze resonates with an intimate familiarity, a reflection of the artist's own spirit.

This self-portrait, stripped of explicit narrative yet saturated with silent whispers of emotional discourse, uses the subtle play of light and shadow to craft a story more powerful than words.

Orchids by the Water

Author: Zhiyin Tan
Date: 17 Nov 2017

Introduction:
Marking the artist's initial exploration of silk fan Gongbi painting, this memorable work, complished in a postgraduate activity at the University of Macau, is adorned with orchids, symbols of gentlemanly virtues.

The choice of the orchid resonates deeply with the artist, as it mirrors a flower linked to her name and signifies an individual of great character and humility in traditional Chinese literature.
