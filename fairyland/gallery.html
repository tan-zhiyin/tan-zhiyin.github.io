The Midnight Gaze

Author: Zhiyin Tan
Date: 03 Jun 2023

Introduction:
Awakening one midnight, I was greeted by the enchanting sight of two cats, Qing Bu Liang, a delightful blend of Neva Masquerade Siberian and Ragdoll, and his charming elder brother, Bai Tang Gao.

Their curious gazes, alternating between me and the insects drawn to the light, brought unexpected warmth and companionship to the midnight hour.

This serene, nocturnal tableau brings a comforting ease that lingers even as dawn breaks.
