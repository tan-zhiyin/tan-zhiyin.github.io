Graph Algorithms

  • Graph Centrality
    Measures the importance of a node within a graph.
  • PageRank
    Algorithm used by Google Search to rank web pages by estimating their importance.
  • Community Detection
    Identifying groups of nodes in a graph that are more densely connected with each other than with the rest of the graph.
  • Graph Embedding
    Converting nodes, edges, or entire graphs into continuous vectors to facilitate machine learning tasks.
  • Graph Clustering
    Grouping nodes based on their connection patterns.
  • Shortest Path Algorithms
    Finding the shortest path between nodes in a graph.
  • Graph Matching
    Finding subgraph isomorphism, or matching nodes of two graphs based on their structure and attributes.
