Querying and Interacting with Knowledge

  • SPARQL (SPARQL Protocol and RDF Query Language):
    A protocol and query language to retrieve and manipulate RDF data.
  • Triple & Quad
    Triple: Basic unit in RDF (Subject, Predicate, Object).
    Quad: Adds a graph context to triples (Graph, Subject, Predicate, Object).
  • Namespace
    Provides a way to avoid collisions between ontology terms and allows for combining different ontologies.
