Welcome to the Computational Linguistics 101, a comprehensive online platform dedicated to democratizing and sharing knowledge on the multifaceted field of computational linguistics.

Housed on a personal website, this page covers core aspects of computational linguistics, from syntax and semantics to natural language processing and beyond. This curated knowledge hub not only provides key insights into foundational concepts but also presents the latest developments in the field, subject to the scope and capability of the curator.

The primary aim is to translate complex terminologies and theories into easy-to-grasp content, presented through diverse and engaging methods.

Your contributions and insights are valued in this collaborative learning journey. Feel free to reach out via email or GitHub to suggest improvements, discuss ideas, or deepen the conversation. Let's together build an inclusive space where computational linguistics is accessible to all.
