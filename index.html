Tan, Zhiyin

written as 谭芷茵 in Chinese,

pronounced /tæn/ /dʒiː/ /jɪːn/ in Mandarin and /tam/ /dzi:/ /jɐn/ in Cantonese

Before obtaining the M.Sc. degree in Computational Linguistics at the Institute of Natural Language Processing of the University of Stuttgart, Germany, I graduated with a M.A. degree in the field of Chinese Linguistics at the Faculty of Arts and Humanities of the University of Macau (a special administrative region of China). Prior to that, I received a B.A. in Chinese Language and Literature from Wuyi University in China.
