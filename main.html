Publications and Projects

Applied Contrastive Learning to Fine-grained Entity Type Classification

A dataset of 32,062 entity descriptions labeled with 228 fine-grained types and 27 coarse-grained types was developed to examine the effectiveness of gain of contrastive learning in classifying entity types across different models with a maximum gain of 17% in accuracy for fine-grained typing, Master thesis, 2023

Text-based Classification of Personality Types

A dataset of 22,647 anonymous posts tagged with the author's MBTI type was developed and used to train the DistilBERT model, yielding an average accuracy of 73.25% for binary classification in each of four dimensions, MLSS, 2021

The use of Cantonese Discourse Markers by Legislative Council Members in Hong Kong and Macau

A spoken Cantonese corpus of 360,000 Chinese characters was developed to explore the correlation between the use of discourse markers and the social background of the speaker (gender, age, education, etc.), Master thesis, WICL-4, 2018

A Comparative Study of Mandarin Pitch Range in Northern and Central Taiwan

Analyzed phonetic data of single words from three men and three women with Praat, revealed Taiwanese Mandarin in Taipei and Taichung can be distinguished by pitch range, NTU Research Program, 2017
